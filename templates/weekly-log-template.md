## Legend
- `*` = Accomplished / Delivered / Unblocked
- `Note` = Gap identified / Risk spotted / Tech Debt found
- `+` = Fixed a previously noted Gap/Risk
- `!` = CRITICAL Blocker (Needs Escalation)

**Examples:**
- `* **[Presales - Discovery] Client A:** Validated technical fit; they use AWS.`
- `* **[Delivery] Proj X:** Note - Client asked for "Encryption at Rest" but it's not in SOW.`
- `* **[Internal]:** Mentored Junior SA on Kafka sizing.`
