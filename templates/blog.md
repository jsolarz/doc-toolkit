### Section 2: [Core Content - Adapt Based on Post Type]

**For Tutorial/How-To Posts:**
- Step-by-step instructions
- Code examples or screenshots
- Common pitfalls to avoid
- Best practices

**For Opinion/Thought Leadership:**
- Your perspective or argument
- Supporting evidence or examples
- Counterarguments and responses
- Implications or predictions

**For Case Studies/Problem-Solving:**
- The challenge or problem faced
- Approach or methodology used
- Implementation details
- Results and outcomes
- Lessons learned

**For Technical Deep Dives:**
- Technical concepts explained
- Architecture or design decisions
- Implementation details
- Trade-offs and alternatives considered
- Performance considerations

**For Personal Experiences:**
- Your journey or experience
- Key moments or decisions
- Challenges encountered
- Insights gained
- How it changed your perspective
